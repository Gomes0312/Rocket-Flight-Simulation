**Resultant Force:**

- *Fr = T - (Da + Dc + P)*
- *a = (T - D - P) / m*
- T = Thrust
- Da = Aerodynamica cone DRAG
- Dc = Capsule DRAG
- P = Weight Force

**Drag:**
  
- *D = 1/2 p v^2 DC A*
- *D = (Dc + Da)*
- p = Air pressure
- v = Speed
- DC = DRAG Coefficient
- A = Area

**Weight Force:**

- *P = mg*
- *g = G M / (h + R)^2*
- M = Earth mass
- G = Universal Gravitational Constant
- R = Earth radius

**Thrust/Mass:**
- *T = Tv - Pr (Tv - Ta)*
- T = Thrust
- Pr = Atmosferic Pressure
- Tv = Thrust in vacuum (0 ATM)
- Ta = Thrust at sea level (1 ATM)
