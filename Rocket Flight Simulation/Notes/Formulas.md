**Resultant Force:**

Fr = T - (Da + Dc + P)

a = (T - D - P) / m

T = Thrust

Da = Aerodynamica cone DRAG

Dc = Capsule DRAG

P = Weight Force

**Drag:**

D = 1/2 p v^2 Dc A

D = (Dc + Da)

p = Air pressure

v = Speed

Dc = DRAG Coefficient

A = Area

**Weight Force:**

P = mg
